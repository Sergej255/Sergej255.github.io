Summoner's Rift

Summoner's Rift is a magical battlefield. In Runeterra Legacy icon Runeterra Legacy, it was the most venerated Field of Justice used by the Institute of War and the League of Legends.
In Blue Team Stag profileicon Institute of War it is an area with remnants of a battle between two competing Empires.

Twisted Treeline

Twisted Treeline was the second classic gameplay map for League of Legends icon League of Legends. It typically features faster,
bloodier matches with an emphasis on ganking and small-scale skirmishes. You achieve victory by pushing your way into the opposing base and destroying the enemy nexus.
